ICC CHAMPIONS TROPHY 2025



The 2025 ICC Champions Trophy will be the ninth edition of the ICC Champions Trophy, a cricket tournament for the eight top-ranked One Day International (ODI) men's national teams organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC) in February and March 2025



#The 2025 ICC Champions Trophy will follow previous editions with two groups of four teams, with the top two teams in each group qualifying for the semi-finals.

#The Teams which are going to compete in the champions trophy 2025 are :-

1. AUSTRALIA

2. INDIA

3. SOUTH AFRICA

4. NEW ZELAND

5. PAKISTAN

6. AFGHANISTAN

7. ENGLAND

8. BANGLADESH
